      I've been a gamer since I was around the age of four years old. Because of my early involvement, I believe it is where I got my interest in making games. I got my first debugging experience at that young age when I was a playtester for an Atari Jaguar game called Battlesphere.

      Years later, I started to learn programming languages such as VB, C/C++, HTML, and C#. The first game design and programming I learned was when I played with Graal 2001's map editor and an engine called BYOND. (I even have one of the fabled "Blue Books"!) My latest endeavour is teaching myself PHP, MySQL, and jQuery.

      I recently graduated from DePaul University with Bachelor's of Science in Game Development, a minor in Animation and another minor in Japanese! 日本語を勉強していますよ！I am following my dreams to live and work in the Japanese game/animation industries.
